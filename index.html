About Me

Hi, I am Towsif. I received my Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science and Engineering (BS CSE) from North South University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Currently, I am working at Samsung Research & Development Institute Bangladesh. My work is mostly related to innovation and research strategies for patent filing and technology management.

Prior to this, I worked as a Research Assistant and a Teaching Assistant at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at North South University. I was also a Lab Instructor at the Department of Mathematics and Physics at North South University.

My primary field of research interest is Artificial Intelligence (AI). Within AI, I am interested in problems related to Computer Vision, Artificial Neural Networks, and Deep Reinforcement Learning. In particular, my research incorporates problems related to machine learning and deep learning.
